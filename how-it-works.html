How Milliways Works

Welcome to Milliways! Our goal is to provide you with a dining experience like no other but before we get started, we'd like to provide you with a little info on how things work here.

Seating

We are technically a first-come, first-served establishment, but there's a catch. You may arrive for any sitting you'd like without prior reservation because you'll book your reservation retrospectively, as it were, when you return to your own time.

Payment

While we accept all major credit cards, the easiest way to pay for your meal is to deposit one penny in a savings account in your own era. When you arrive at the End of Time, the operation of compound interest means that your delicious meal will already be paid for.

Location

Travel to and from our establishment often involves convoluted grammatical structures, such as those addressed in Dan Streetmentioner's "Time Traveler's Handbook of 1001 Tense Formations". We keep a number of copies on-hand as a reference, should you need to look something up at any point during your stay with us. Also, it is not unusual for guests to be a little disorientated by the temporal displacement that occurs when entering or exiting Milliways.

We understand that many of the facts surrounding our restaurant are plainly impossible. This is why we hired the best of the best, the advertising executives of the star system of Bastablon, and they came up with our slogan: "If you've done six impossible things this morning, why not round it off with breakfast at Milliways, the Restaurant at the End of the Universe."
